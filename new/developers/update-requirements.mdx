title: "Update Requirements"
description: "Requiremnets for updating smart contracts with custom code."
---

The Trusset protocol requires to keep standartized token interfaces to work with all services. These interfaes depend on the underlying asset type / token standard that is used. For updating the ERC-3463 standard, please visit the official T-Rex documentation. 

<Tip>Updating an asset-specific smart contract will (legally) require an audit for the changes.</Tip>

## Audits
Auditing your contract updates is a crucial part of compliant operations. We recommend [Softstack](https://softstack.io/) for reliable audits.
