# Meet Trusset
Trusset connects verified asset issuers directly to businesses and individuals. Our platform lets you turn your existing assets into fractional tokens on the blockchain, manage them, trade them, and access liquidity in a compliant ecosystem. 

# Our Mission
We believe you should control your own assets without relying on profit-driven organizations or unstable third-party systems.
