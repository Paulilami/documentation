Trusset is a next-generation protocol for financial institutions that turns any asset into liquid collateral. We provide next-generation infrastructure APIs for tokenization, trading, and lending. This allows our customers to independently replicate their existing capital markets on the blockchain and synchronizing operations directly from their backend.

We think that tokenization is pointless unless the asset has utility. Our credit-first architecture makes every asset instantly borrowable. Holdings become active collateral. Liquidity becomes automatic, a new revenue engine powered by continuous credit markets.

Start with what matters to you: understand the infrastructure, explore integration paths, review our smart contract licensing, or request access to build.
