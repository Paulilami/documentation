title: "Intro to Trusset"
---

Trusset is an infrastructure company for financial institutions. We turn static holdings (stocks, commodities, real estate, and more) into programmable instruments that trade around the clock, settle instantly, and serve as loan collateral, with compliance enforced automatically at the contract level.

At the core is a credit-first architecture: every tokenized asset becomes immediately eligible as collateral for automated credit lines, unlocking liquidity that would otherwise sit idle. Risk parameters, liquidation thresholds, and investor eligibility are defined once and enforced continuously by smart contracts, replacing manual underwriting and reconciliation. Clients retain full ownership of their contracts, data, and governance.

Start with what matters to you: understand the infrastructure, explore integration paths, review our smart contract licensing, or request access to build.
