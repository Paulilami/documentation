---
title: 'Add Liquidity'
api: 'POST /api/balancer/v1/pools/{poolId}/liquidity/add'
---

Add liquidity to an existing pool to earn trading fees and BPT tokens.

<ParamField path="poolId" type="string" required>
  The unique identifier of the pool
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="maxAmountsIn" type="array" required>
  Maximum amounts of each token to add [stockTokenAmount, pairedTokenAmount]
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="minBptAmountOut" type="string" default="0">
  Minimum BPT tokens to receive (slippage protection)
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="recipient" type="string">
  Address to receive BPT tokens (defaults to sender)
</ParamField>
