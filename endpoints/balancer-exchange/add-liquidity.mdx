---
title: 'Add Liquidity'
api: 'POST /api/balancer-exchange/pools/{poolId}/liquidity/add'
---

Add liquidity to a Balancer pool. Users provide both tokens in proportion to earn trading fees.

<ParamField path="poolId" type="string" required>
  The unique identifier of the pool
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="amounts" type="array" required>
  Array of token amounts to add [stockTokenAmount, pairedTokenAmount]
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="minLpTokens" type="string">
  Minimum LP tokens to receive (slippage protection)
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="userAddress" type="string" required>
  The Ethereum address of the liquidity provider
</ParamField>

<RequestExample>

    "userAddress": "0x742d35Cc6634C0532925a3b844Bc9e7595f5b5d4"
