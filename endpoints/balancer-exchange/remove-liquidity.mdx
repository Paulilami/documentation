---
title: 'Remove Liquidity'
api: 'POST /api/balancer/pools/{poolId}/liquidity/remove'
---

Remove liquidity from a Balancer pool by burning LP tokens and receiving underlying tokens.

<ParamField path="poolId" type="string" required>
  The unique identifier of the pool
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="lpTokens" type="string" required>
  Amount of LP tokens to burn
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="minAmountsOut" type="array">
  Minimum amounts of underlying tokens to receive [minStockTokens, minPairedTokens]
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="userAddress" type="string" required>
  The Ethereum address of the liquidity provider
</ParamField>

<RequestExample>
```bash cURL
curl -X POST "https://trusser-issuer-backend.vercel.app/api/balancer/pools/0x1234567890abcdef1234567890abcdef1234567890abcdef1234567890abcdef12/liquidity/remove" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -H "x-api-key: YOUR_API_KEY" \
  -d '{
    "lpTokens": "50",
    "minAmountsOut": ["45", "4500"],
    "userAddress": "0x742d35Cc6634C0532925a3b844Bc9e7595f5b5d4"
  }'
```
