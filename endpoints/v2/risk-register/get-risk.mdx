---
title: 'Get Risk Record'
api: 'GET /api/v2/risk/assets/{assetAddress}'
---

Retrieve the current risk data for a specific tokenized asset.

## Path Parameters

<ParamField path="assetAddress" type="string" required>
  The token contract address
</ParamField>

## Response Fields

<ResponseField name="id" type="string">Unique record identifier</ResponseField>
<ResponseField name="assetAddress" type="string">Token contract address</ResponseField>
<ResponseField name="riskScore" type="number">Current risk score (1-100)</ResponseField>
<ResponseField name="riskGrade" type="string">Letter grade (A-F)</ResponseField>
<ResponseField name="category" type="string">Risk category</ResponseField>
<ResponseField name="metadata" type="object">Issuer-specific details</ResponseField>
<ResponseField name="updatedAt" type="string">Last update timestamp</ResponseField>
