---
title: 'Grant Role'
api: 'POST /api/stablecoin/grant-role'
---

Grant minter or burner role to an address for stablecoin operations.

<ParamField body="stablecoinAddress" type="string" required>
  Address of the stablecoin contract
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="account" type="string" required>
  Address to grant role to
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="role" type="string" required>
  Role to grant: "minter" or "burner"
</ParamField>

<RequestExample>

```bash cURL
curl -X POST "https://trusser-issuer-backend.vercel.app/api/stablecoin/grant-role" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -H "x-api-key: YOUR_API_KEY" \
  -d '{
    "stablecoinAddress": "0xf265EB7723d5493722e92C9dBF4E993d7b36D903",
    "account": "0x742d35Cc6634C0532925a3b844Bc9e7595f5b5d4",
    "role": "minter"
  }'
```
