---
title: 'Recover Funds'
api: 'POST /api/stablecoin/recover-funds'
---

Recover funds from a frozen account for regulatory compliance. Requires recovery role and the source account must be frozen.

<ParamField body="stablecoinAddress" type="string" required>
  Address of the stablecoin contract
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="from" type="string" required>
  Frozen address to recover funds from
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="to" type="string" required>
  Address to transfer recovered funds to
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="amount" type="string" required>
  Amount of tokens to recover
</ParamField>

<RequestExample>

```bash cURL

  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -H "x-api-key: YOUR_API_KEY" \
  -d '{
    "stablecoinAddress": "0xBDdEB75beb9A604dE5C82495f1331E0A4E7b2f15",
    "from": "0x742d35Cc6634C0532925a3b844Bc9e7595f5b5d4",

  }'
```
