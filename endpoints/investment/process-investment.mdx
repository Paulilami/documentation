---
title: 'Process Investment'
api: 'POST /api/v1/investment/invest'
---

Process an investment for a token sale.

## Request Body

<ParamField body="tokenAddress" type="string" required>
  ERC20 token contract address
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="investorAddress" type="string" required>
  Ethereum address of the investor
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="amount" type="number" required>
  Investment amount in EUR
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="paymentMethod" type="string" default="crypto">
  Payment method: "crypto" or "bank"
</ParamField>

<RequestExample>
```bash cURL

  -H "x-instance-id: YOUR_INSTANCE_ID" \
```
