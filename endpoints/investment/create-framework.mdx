---
title: 'Create Framework'
api: 'POST /api/v1/investment/framework'
---

Configure the legal and operational framework for token offerings within your bank instance.

## Request Body

<ParamField body="legalType" type="string" default="profit-participation">
  Legal structure type for token offerings
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="maxInvestors" type="number" required>
  Maximum number of investors allowed per offering (1-10,000)
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="maxRaiseAmount" type="number" required>
  Maximum amount that can be raised without a prospectus (in EUR)
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="minInvestment" type="number" default="100">
  Minimum investment amount per investor (in EUR)
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="maxInvestment" type="number" default="1000000">
  Maximum investment amount per investor (in EUR)
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="allowPrivateMarkets" type="boolean" default="true">
  Enable secondary market trading
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="transactionFee" type="number" default="100">
  Transaction fee in basis points (0-1000)
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="prospectusThreshold" type="number" default="8000000">
  Amount threshold requiring a prospectus (in EUR)
</ParamField>
