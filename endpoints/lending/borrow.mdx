---
title: 'Borrow from Pool'
api: 'POST /api/lending/borrow'
---

Borrow tokens from a lending pool by providing collateral. The loan is overcollateralized and monitored via health factor.

<ParamField body="poolAddress" type="string" required>
  Address of the lending pool contract
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="borrowAmount" type="string" required>
  Amount of tokens to borrow
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="collateralAmount" type="string" required>
  Amount of collateral tokens to provide
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="userAddress" type="string" required>
  Address of the borrower
</ParamField>
