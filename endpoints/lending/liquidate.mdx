---
title: 'Liquidate Position'
api: 'POST /api/lending/liquidate'
---

Liquidate an undercollateralized position by repaying debt and seizing collateral. Available when health factor drops below 1.0.

<ParamField body="poolAddress" type="string" required>
  Address of the lending pool contract
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="borrowerAddress" type="string" required>
  Address of the borrower to liquidate
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="repayAmount" type="string" required>
  Amount of debt to repay (max 50% of total debt)
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="liquidatorAddress" type="string" required>
  Address of the liquidator executing the liquidation
</ParamField>
