---
title: 'Repay Loan'
api: 'POST /api/lending/repay'
---

Repay borrowed tokens to reduce debt and improve health factor. Full repayment releases all collateral.

<ParamField body="poolAddress" type="string" required>
  Address of the lending pool contract
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="amount" type="string" required>
  Amount of tokens to repay
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="userAddress" type="string" required>
  Address of the borrower repaying the loan
</ParamField>
